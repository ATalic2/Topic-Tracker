# Privacy Policy for Topic Tracker

_Last updated: March 2026_

## Overview

Topic Tracker is designed with privacy in mind. All data is stored locally on your device and is never collected, transmitted, or shared with any third party.

## Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored on your device using Chrome's built-in storage API:

- URLs and page titles of visited pages that match your topic rules
- Your topic groups, names, colors, and matching rules
- Extension settings such as deduplication window, expiry period, and tracking preferences

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible by the extension itself.

## External Requests

Topic Tracker makes one type of external request:

- **Favicons** — domain names of saved URLs are sent to Google's favicon service (`https://www.google.com/s2/favicons`) solely to display icons next to saved URLs in the popup. No other data is transmitted.

## Data Removal

- Individual URLs can be removed at any time from the extension popup
- All saved URLs can be cleared from the extension settings
- Uninstalling the extension permanently removes all locally stored data
